◆ mode

ProfileSolverMode BSMPT::VacuumProfileNS::VacuumProfile::mode = ProfileSolverMode::Deriv

Method used to solve the field equation.

  • Field - Fixed \( \vec\phi(z \to \infty) = \vec\phi_f \) and \( \vec\phi(z \to -\infty) = \vec\phi_t \)
  • Deriv - Fixes \frac{d\vec\phi}{dz}(|z| \to \infty) = 0
